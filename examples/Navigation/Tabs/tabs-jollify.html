The word ‘Jollify’ means to make something joyful, it was inspired by Jupiter: The bringer of Jollity by Gustav Holst. So the idea is that this app will make your presentation a more joyful and pleasant experience for your audience. The Dolphin on the logo was inspired when in 2018 NASA released a picture of a Dolphin shaped cloud on Jupiter.
